Frequently Asked Questions
How does the Gemini AI assistant work?expand_more
The Gemini AI assistant connects to your linked data sources (BigQuery, IoT, APIs) and processes natural language queries using Google's Gemini 2.0 Flash model. It can generate charts, write SQL, summarize reports, and suggest actions in real-time.
What data sources can I connect?expand_more
CommunityPulse AI supports BigQuery, Google Cloud Storage, Firebase, PostgreSQL, MySQL, REST APIs, CSV/Excel file uploads, IoT streaming data, and Looker dashboards. More connectors are added monthly.
How accurate are the predictions?expand_more
Our ML models trained on 18,700+ community datasets achieve 87–94% accuracy depending on the prediction domain. Traffic prediction is our highest accuracy model at 94%, while newer domains like disease spread achieve ~73% accuracy and improve over time.
Is my data secure and private?expand_more
Yes. All data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We comply with GDPR, ISO 27001, and SOC 2 Type II. Your data never leaves your selected Google Cloud region and is never used to train shared models.
Can I export reports to PowerPoint or PDF?expand_more
Yes! The Reports module supports one-click export to PDF, Excel, CSV, and PowerPoint. AI-generated executive summaries are automatically formatted for presentation. You can also schedule automated weekly/monthly report delivery by email.
